Free Texas

Free Texas is a web app designed for the fun loving but frugal minded folks in Texas. It lets you search for free events in the Food/Drink and Music category for any date. Just select the city you want to go out in, pick your date, and you will see all events for that day! It also displays the weather to let you know how to prepare for the event.

Free Texas was designed using Html5/Css3, Bootstrap, Javascript, jQuery, Ajax, along with the Eventbrite Api and Open Weather Map Api.

I was the UX Designer and Lead JavaScript Engineer on the project. I also came up with the idea for the project, designed the website through two iterations, and helped with connecting the Eventbrite API. Our team was a group of three, and we worked together in writing pieces of the JavaScript code. I also designed and wrote the Chrome Extension myself.


Visit the website here, and have a fun free day in Texas!
